Software developer

École 42 : 2019 - 2020

42 is a pioneering and innovative coding school that operates with a unique educational model, emphasizing peer-to-peer learning and project-based pedagogy. Founded in Paris, France, by Xavier Niel, 42 has expanded globally, offering its no-tuition programming education to students from all walks of life. The school distinguishes itself by having no traditional teachers or classrooms, and instead, students learn through completing coding projects and collaborating with their peers. This self-directed learning environment is designed to foster software development skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities, preparing students for careers in the tech industry. 42's curriculum covers various aspects of computer science, including algorithms, data structures, databases, web development, and cybersecurity.
