Educational fablab manager

emlyon makers' lab : 2019 - 2023

The emlyon business school features unique makers' labs in Écully, Paris, and Saint-Étienne, serving its entire ecosystem with facilities for rapid prototyping. These labs, inspired by the fablab model, encourage creativity and practical learning across design, web, IoT, AI, and digital fabrication. They provide machinery, materials, electronics, software, and expert support, complemented by online tutorials, events, and courses.
As educational fablab manager, my role was to imagine, create and distribute the Prototyping-with-Fab course, on the subject of object prototyping using digital fabrication tools. This course was offered in both French and English across all three campuses, reaching over 1000 students annually. Additionally, I contributed to collaborative events with corporate partners like Mazars and Renault Trucks, employing Design Sprint methodologies. These events spanned three days, during which we prototyped projects through various modules and the contributions of external experts.

My key responsibilities in this role included:

  • Creation of pedagogical content.
  • Recruiting and training instructors.
  • Creation and monitoring of evaluation criteria.
  • Facilitation and animation of Design Thinking training for executives.
  • Preparation and facilitation of workshops based on digital fabrication tools.
  • Student project management.
  • Machine maintenance.
